The moment has come, finally! After several years of hard work, [our new critical edition](http://wydawnictwo.ibl.waw.pl/serie-wydawnicze/dziela-wszystkie-jana-kochanowskiego) of Jan Kochanowski's _Epigrams_ is out! At the moment, vol. 1 is available in print, and vol. 2 will come very soon, hopefully.
